What is Atom Economy?

Atom economy measures how efficiently reactants are converted into useful products. Higher atom economy means less waste and more sustainable chemical processes.

  • 90%+ → Excellent (Green Chemistry Ideal)
  • 70–89% → Good
  • 50–69% → Average
  • Below 50% → Poor (High waste)
